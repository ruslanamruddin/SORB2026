About the Conference

The Southern Ontario Reproductive Biology (SORB) Conference is an annual, one-day scientific meeting that brings together researchers, clinicians, and trainees from across Ontario and beyond to share the latest advances in reproductive biology.

Now in its 53rd year, SORB provides a welcoming and collegial environment for graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and faculty to present their work through oral and poster presentations. The conference rotates among host universities each year, fostering collaboration across institutions.

SORB 2026 is proudly hosted by Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario. We look forward to continuing this tradition of scientific excellence and community.
