Queen's University — Kingston, Ontario

Queen's University is situated on traditional Anishinaabe and Haudenosaunee territory. We are grateful to be able to live, learn, and work on these lands. We acknowledge the enduring presence of Indigenous peoples on this land, and we give thanks for their continued stewardship.

Queen's University

Queen's University is one of Canada's leading research-intensive institutions, with internationally recognized programs in health sciences, biomedical research, and the life sciences. Located in the vibrant city of Kingston along the shores of Lake Ontario, Queen's provides an ideal setting for scholarly exchange.

Getting to Kingston

By Car

Kingston is located along Highway 401, approximately 2.5 hours from Toronto and 2 hours from Ottawa.

By Train

VIA Rail provides regular service to Kingston station, a short taxi or bus ride from campus.

By Air

The nearest major airports are Kingston Norman Rogers Airport (YGK) and Ottawa Macdonald-Cartier International Airport (YOW).
