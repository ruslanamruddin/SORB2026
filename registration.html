For Presenters

Presenter Guidelines

Abstract Submission

Abstracts should be a maximum of 300 words and should be structured to include:

  • Title, author(s), and affiliations
  • Background and objectives
  • Methods
  • Results
  • Conclusion
  • Funding

Oral Presentations

  • 10-minute presentation followed by 3–5 minutes of Q&A
  • Selected speakers will be asked to submit their PowerPoint slides prior to the conference
  • Presenters should arrive early to test A/V equipment

Poster Presentations

  • Maximum size: 122 × 92 cm (48 × 36 in), landscape orientation
  • Title and authors clearly visible at top
  • Text should be readable from 3 metres away
  • Posters must be pre-printed; organizers provide boards and mounting materials

Flash Talk

  • 1 slide, 3-minute talk providing an overview of research
  • Flash talk participants will also prepare and present a poster during the poster session (see specifications above for poster presentations)

Registration

Registration is now open. The deadline to register is Friday, May 1, 2026.

Registration through the link is required for all attendees. If you are presenting a talk, flash talk, or poster — or attending the meeting only — please register.

Registration and payment can only be completed on a person-by-person basis; we are unable to coordinate group payments.
